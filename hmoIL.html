Clinical Decision Support

Autonomous Physiological Telemetry

PeakIQ passively ingests data from patients' existing wearable devices, identifying autonomic stress 24-48 hours before physical symptoms manifest.

  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV): Detects parasympathetic suppression, acting as an early indicator for immune compromise and burnout.
  • Acute:Chronic Workload Ratio (ACWR): A biomechanical risk algorithm that predicts tissue structure failure with 79% accuracy when ratios exceed the 1.5 danger threshold.
  • Proactive Nudge: Dispatches an automated, personalized rest protocol directly to the patient, intercepting the event before clinical intervention is required.
IT Architecture

Frictionless EMR Integration

Operating strictly under Ministry of Health guidelines for AI, maintaining human-in-the-loop oversight with zero workflow disruption.

1. Secure Data Ingestion
Wearable telemetry is processed in a sovereign cloud. Only actionable threshold breaches (e.g., ACWR > 1.5) generate an HL7/FHIR compliant alert sent to the HMO’s middleware (e.g., C-Pi).
2. EMR Surface Layer
The HMO's platform cross-references the alert with the patient's history and surfaces an actionable recommendation directly inside the native Clicks EMR. No external dashboards required.
